License

This is a very simple description for the LSB license.

For individuals

Currently LSB is free for individuals, including personal, commercial, and non-profit use. To support the development and help the author to keep improving on the LSB, you can volunterially pay the author to show your support. Together with your payment, please send an email to the official email leave DOT script DOT behind AT outlook DOT com, share what you have paid for the LSB. We will record your information. In future, there could be new features released to this group of users before they are available publicly.

If you are an individual who wants to use the LSB for commercial purpose, it is allowed and free of charge under the current license. Meanwhile you are strongly suggested to pay what you think that the LSB values and what you can afford for to the author. This can help the author to keep adding new features into the LSB application and making the LSB better. The author reserves the right to change the license from free of charge to payment required for commercial uses.

For businesses

If you represent a commerical company and want to use the LSB application in your organization, please contact leave DOT script DOT behind AT outlook DOT com for detailed prices and license information. If you want to get the author's help to create dedicated LSB views for your business, please contact the author at the same email.
