Enumeration HorizontalAlignment

Description

This is the enumeration type used for the align attribute in the Column, indicates how the content in the column will be aligned horizontally. The supported values in this type are,

  • Center - The content in the column will be center aligned.
  • Left - The content in the column will be left aligned.
  • Right - The content in the column will be right aligned.

Supported controls:

  • ListView
  • TreeListView
