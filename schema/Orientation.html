Enumeration Orientation

Description

This is the enumeration type used for the orientation attribute in the SplitLayout, indicates the layout direction of the two panes in the SplitLayout. The supported values in this type are,

  • Horizontal - The two panes are placed horizontally, one in left and one in right.
  • Vertical - The two panes are placed vertically, on in top and one in bottom.

Supported controls:

  • SplitLayout
